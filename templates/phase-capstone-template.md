## Evaluation Criteria

Your capstone will be self-evaluated and optionally peer-reviewed using this rubric:

| Criterion | Weight | Excellent | Good | Needs Work |

| Correctness | 35% | Implementation is mathematically correct and produces expected results | Minor issues but core is correct | Significant errors |
